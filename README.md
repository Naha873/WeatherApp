# WeatherApp

## Purpose of the product

Relying solely on third-party weather apps can present challenges for a company. Data privacy concerns arise when sensitive user location data is shared with external providers. Furthermore, third-party apps may not offer the specific features or level of customisation required for internal use cases, such as hyperlocal forecasts for company sites or integration with existing internal systems. Building an in-house weather app addresses these issues. While the initial prototype may focus on core functionalities like displaying current weather conditions, it serves as a foundation for future enhancements.

This includes features such as severe weather alerts and personalised notifications, which can significantly improve employee safety and operational efficiency. The app can be tailored to specific needs, offering features like real-time weather updates, and integration with internal systems for improved operational efficiency. This benefits various departments, including facilities management (for maintenance planning), event planning (for outdoor event decisions), and even employee well-being (for informing employees about extreme weather conditions). The app can be particularly useful in locations prone to severe weather events, enabling proactive measures to ensure employee safety and minimize disruptions to business operations.
